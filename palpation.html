Palpation.

A good knowledge of anatomy is essential for accurate palpation. Palpation can help you locate the painful structure and identify any inflammation or scar tissue, other things you may look for when palpating are:

  • Production of pain
  • Temperature
  • Skin moisture
  • Oedema/effusion
  • Muscle spasm
  • Tenderness
  • Bony prominence
  • Trigger points
  • Thickening
