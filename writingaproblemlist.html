Writing A Problem List.

Example problem list:

  1. Pain - VAS sharp pain 6/10 and constant ache 1-3/10.
  2. ROM - Limited active extension due to muscle pain.
  3. Muscle - Pain on active wrist extension or stretch of wrist extensors.
  4. Nerve - Altered skin sensation lateral aspect of thumb.
  5. Nerve - Reduced neural mobility for the radial nerve.
  6. ADL - Difficulty opening jars, dressing, writing, typing.
  7. Work - Difficulty playing piano.
  8. Sport - Unable to play tennis.
