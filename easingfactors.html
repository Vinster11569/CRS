Easing Factors.

Easing factors are movements, activities of daily living or postures that reduce your patient’s symptoms. It is important to ask for timings from your patient – this will allow you to work out the irritability of the condition.

Consider these examples:

e.g. getting up and moving around eases my pain.
e.g. getting up and moving around eases my pain after about ½ hour.
e.g. getting up and moving around eases my pain immediately.
