Writing SMART Goals.

S = Specific
M = Measureable
A = Achievable / Actionable
R = Realistic / Relevant
T = Timed

Example SMART Goals:

  1. Pain free VAS 0/10 6/52
  2. No Swelling of the ankle so L=R in 2/52
  3. FROM right ankle 4/52
  4. Negative ATFL test 6/52
  5. Peroneals OS 4/5 in 8/52 - OS 5/5 in 3/12
  6. Walking with assistance of 2 sticks 2/52, No aid 4/52, PWB-FWB. Safety on stairs with 2 e/c immediately. Safety on stairs - ongoing as NWB - PWB - FWB
  7. Safe to travel to work 1/52. Restart Marathon training 8-12/52.
